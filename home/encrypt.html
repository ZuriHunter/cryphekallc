Encryption

Step 1

Enter the a key that is only unique to the recipient and yourself. Then enter your desired message or upload a file to your liking that you wish to encrypt.

Step 2

After you completed your message, select the options towards the left in regards to the message being viewed by a specific device or a group of people and if you wish to have a time deadling for the message.

Step 3

Select the "Encrypt" button, to convert the message into an encryption and select the "Copy" button to copy the newly encrypted message. From there you can place the encrypted message in any document or sending service. It will be on the recipient to come back to our service to decrypt it with the key that you provided.
